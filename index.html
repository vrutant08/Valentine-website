Hey my Giasemi!

You know that today, the world is wrapped in shades of roses 🌹 and chocolates 🍫
but for me it's not only about roses 🌹 or chocolates 🍫.
It's about this moment, writing you, pouring out every corner of my heart
and reminding you that you've been the most vibrant color in my life.

Being with you and proposing you was the best decision I’ve ever made in my life,
I’m sorry I have said I don’t like being with you many times when I was angry at you,
but I never felt, I never ever had a thought passing my mind that, “I should not have been with you”
ever since I met you, everything started making more sense,
I found myself and my soul resonating with you,
and I started doing the things and being more intrigued with them which resonated with my soul.
You make me feel, some of the feelings I didn’t even realize I was capable of having.
Some of them are foreign to me, so I take little bit more time to recognize them.
Each time I talk to you I feel my words flow by themselves; I don’t need to think about them,
my mind, my body feels free with you.
When I think about how you look at me, how our fingers fit together,
how you feel in my arms, your lips 💋 against mine everything feels just so right.
You must be thinking why I’m saying this when I haven’t even felt how half of them feels,
but before you I only thought that I would have them with my life partner,
I met you in long distance but you still made me feel these feelings with your immense intimacy.
My heart ❤️ recognizes yours, and I swear it beats differently when I’m with you.

I know recently you told me, “I don’t know if you miss me the way I miss you”
I haven’t wrote things this way because I was invested in many other things that kept my mind busy.
But for the answer of your worry I will say, when I miss you, you're all around me,
with me, in me, and alongside me… yet still so far away.
I see you in the waters of the lake, and I drown in your love as it washes over me,
I see you in the sun rays coming through the clouds.
This is still not enough to say how much I think about you and how much I want you by my side.
I’m able to imagine the next 60 years of my life, amidst the hustle of life, you are always with me.
I hear your encouraging words, I can feel the familiarity of our souls, and I know that I’m home.
You provide me with the sense of peace and acceptance.
But when I think about all the love and warmth you’ve poured into my life,
I can’t help but feel that I’ve been holding the world’s most precious gift in my hands.
And like what kind of love would it be if I didn’t want to give you that same peace?
I want to be your strength, your light, your peace just as you’ve been mine.
I see the cracks I’ve left behind in you, the ones I wish I could have filled with love instead of pain,
I can’t erase the past but as I’ve given you a promise, I want to spend the rest of my life making sure your heart feels full.

My Giasemi, I can’t stop the flow of my tears when I’m writing this letter to you,
but really, all of these feelings that I have described above, I never thought I would feel them for someone…
Thank you for giving me power, for giving me an opportunity to feel this way, and for giving me a great life,
thank you for being my valentine.
I want to keep on embracing our relationship with you
and make even more loving memories that we will cherish in our hearts forever.
